Privacy Policy

At FRIDGO, your privacy matters. We built this platform to help you discover ingredients and recipes from your fridge while keeping your experience simple, useful, and respectful of your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you use FRIDGO, we may process the photos you upload, ingredient-related input, and basic usage information such as the actions you take on the website. We do not ask for sensitive personal data unless you choose to share it voluntarily.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze uploaded images and identify ingredients.
  • To generate recipe suggestions and cooking recommendations.
  • To improve the reliability and quality of the AI experience.
  • To protect the platform from misuse, abuse, or suspicious activity.

3. Data Storage and Protection

We aim to minimize data retention and avoid storing your images permanently. Any uploaded content is handled with care and used only for the purpose of providing the requested service. We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

4. Your Choices

You can choose not to upload images or not to use certain features. If you are concerned about your data, you may contact us and request clarification about how your information is handled.

5. Third-Party Services

FRIDGO may rely on external AI services or hosting providers to deliver part of its functionality. These third parties are expected to follow appropriate security and confidentiality practices.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect improvements, legal requirements, or new features. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised date.

By continuing to use FRIDGO, you agree to the latest version of this policy.
